About Us

Excelling in the realm of International trade, SUNTEX is at the forefront of supplying premium quality Textile raw materials, Chemicals and Agro products across the global market. The company plays a pivotal role, acting as an intermediary between vendors and clients, adding value to the service offered. With a rich legacy of over a decade, the company has burgeoned to be among the few eminent players transforming the industry with its superior quality products. Acclaimed to be one of the fastest growing supplier world-wide, we have achieved a reputation of high stature in the industry. Today, the company’s rapid expansion emanates from years of experience and expertise of professionals who believe in creating sustainable value for all its business partners. Sourced from the most reliable and authentic vendors, the company aims to cater clients with world class products that are competitively priced without compromising on the quality of products. Further, as we plan to diversify our operations, we intend to grow our client base in the most lucrative markets by leveraging opportunities in the years to come.
